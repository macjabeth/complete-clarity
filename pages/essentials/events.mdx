## Organisers

Players who are interested in running events may be given the `Event Organizer` role on Discord, which has nearly admin powers in the `#company-events` channel.

These players are responsible for gauging and gathering interest, and planning and running company events.

## Types of Events

- Expeditions
- Exploration
- PvP Combat
- Territory/Wars
